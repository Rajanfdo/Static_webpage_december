About FreshCatch

Your trusted partner for fresh seafood since 2018

Our Story

FreshCatch was born from a simple idea: everyone deserves access to fresh, high-quality seafood delivered right to their doorstep. Founded in 2018 by a group of fishing enthusiasts and tech entrepreneurs in Chennai, we've grown from a small local operation to one of the most trusted seafood delivery services in Tamil Nadu.

Our journey began at the Marina Beach fish market, where we saw firsthand the incredible variety of fresh catch available daily. We also noticed that many people didn't have the time or expertise to select the best quality fish. That's when we decided to bridge this gap.

Today, we work directly with local fishermen and sustainable fishing operations to bring you the freshest seafood possible. Every morning, our team personally selects the best catch of the day, ensuring that only premium quality fish reaches your table.
